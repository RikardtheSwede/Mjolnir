MJÖLNIR DOM - OPERATOR MANUAL

GENERAL CONTROLS

  • Middle Click: Recenter DOM to current price.
  • Scroll Wheel: Move DOM up/down.
  • Ctrl + Scroll: Zoom in/out.

PRICE COLUMN

  • Right Click: EMERGENCY FLATTEN ALL.

BID & ASK COLUMNS

  • Left Click: Sniper Entry (Join Bid/Ask).

BUY & SEL COLUMNS (ACTION MODE)

Used when Flat, or adding to position.

  • Left Click: Place LMT Entry.
  • Alt + Left Click: Place STP Entry.
  • Left Click (on order): Add quantity (Scale).
  • Right Click: Cancel Entry.
  • Ctrl + Left Click: Move Anchor Group.

BUY & SEL COLUMNS (PROTECTION MODE)

Used to manage active position.

  • Left Click: Move Stop Loss (SL).
  • Ctrl + Left Click: Move Take Profit (TP).
  • Right Click: Cancel TP.

LVL COLUMN

  • Left Click (Empty Space): Add "MAN" level.
  • Left Click (On Level): Edit label.
  • Right Click: Delete level.
  • Shift + Right Click: Clear ALL levels.
